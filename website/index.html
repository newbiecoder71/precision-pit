Recommended Hero Video Shot List

  • Driver or crew member holding a phone with Precision Pit open
  • Dirt or asphalt track visible behind them with cars in motion
  • Close-up angle first, then a wider trackside shot
  • Short clips showing Dashboard, Race Night, and Setup screens

Best Format

  • Portrait and landscape versions if possible
  • 10 to 20 seconds is enough for a homepage hero loop
  • Muted background video with a separate voiceover tutorial
  • Use the video as the top hero once you have the final footage
